CODE MENTOR

Computing resources tailored to your degree

CodeMentor is a collection of resources designed to help college students learn topics taught in their computing classes.

SIGN UP NOW

Features

By collecting credible resources on topics taught in each class, you can come to one place to learn the material you need to know to be successful. And you can track your progress and take notes without leaving the site.
Pretty cool, right?

Resources for specific courses

Because CodeMentor is designed for students by students, we understand the importance of using resources that will help in your courses. Intro to Programming classes are different across universities, so we create a unique set of resources for each course at each university.

Track your progress

CodeMentor allows you to check off which links and activities you have already completed so you donâ€™t have to waste time trying to find the last article you read or quiz you passed.

Take notes within the site

Switching back and forth between an article and whatever application you use to take notes is one of the most frustrating parts of learning online. CodeMentor has built-in note taking functionality that solves that problem, allowing you to take notes while you read and saving all your notes within the site.
